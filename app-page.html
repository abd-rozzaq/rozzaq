App Design for Social Fitness "TBD"

The main purpose of this app is a social fitness app where a group of close friends (up to 6) individuals can work out together via a two way video work out system within a group called a Pod. The app will connect to apple watch or Fibit, so live health stats like heart rate, % of max heart rate, calories burned etc. will be overlaid with each participants video as they work out.

Client Requirements

Clients want a design that is easy to understand and can show the results of the workout they have done, whether in days, months, and years.

The User & Audience

People who want fitness training without having to bother going to the gym, and can do workouts with their friends together.

Role & Responsibility

I act as UI Designer, who designs the interface of this web app.

Wireframe

The main screen is your work out area with a live audio/video conference feed for each of the 6 (or fewer) individuals within the workout pod. There can be 3 modes - collaborative, competitive & instructor.

Collaborative is just friends working out together

Competitive can be friends or a pod that you join up with

Instructor mode has an instructor always at the top
